Structural Tags (HEADER, NAV, MAIN, FOOTER)

What goes in the header?

The header element serves as a source for either introductory content or navigational links that facilitate movement around the website.

What goes in the Nav? Can the Nav go in the header? Does it have to go in the header? Does the Nav contain links to another website?

A specific purpose of the nav element is to create navigational links, which aid users in navigating within the website.

What goes in Main?

The main element is the core content of your webpage, representing the main section that is unique to the page.

What goes in the Footer?

The element of footer stands for the content of footer part of page.
