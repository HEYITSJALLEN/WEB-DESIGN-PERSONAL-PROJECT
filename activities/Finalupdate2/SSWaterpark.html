Seven Seas Adventure Park


Seven Seas Waterpark and Resort, located in Opol, Misamis Oriental, near Cagayan de Oro City, is a premier destination for adventure and relaxation in Northern Mindanao. Known as the largest pirate-themed waterpark in the Philippines, it features world-class attractions, including thrilling waterslides, a wave pool, and a lazy river.
