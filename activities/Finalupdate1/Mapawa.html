Mapawa


Mapawa Nature Park, located in Malasag Cugman, Cagayan de Oro City, is a haven for adventure seekers and nature lovers alike. This serene destination offers a wide range of activities, from horseback riding and camping to canyoneering and zip-lining. Visitors can enjoy picturesque waterfalls, verdant hiking trails, and breathtaking views that showcase the park's unspoiled beauty. Whether you're seeking a thrilling outdoor adventure or a peaceful retreat in nature, Mapawa Nature Park provides a perfect escape from the hustle and bustle of city life.
