Mapawa


Mapawa Nature Park, located in Malasag Cugman, Cagayan de Oro City, is a paradise for adventure enthusiasts and nature lovers. This tranquil destination offers a variety of activities, including horseback riding, camping, canyoneering, and zip-lining. Visitors can explore scenic waterfalls, lush hiking trails, and breathtaking landscapes that highlight the park’s pristine beauty. Whether you’re craving an adrenaline-pumping adventure or a serene escape into nature, Mapawa Nature Park is the perfect retreat from the hustle and bustle of city life.
