My Interests - Guardians of the Galaxy

Groot

Groot is a Flora collosus, the prophesized hero of Drez-Lar, and the accomplice of Rocket. Together, the pair had traveled the galaxy picking up bounties until they met Star-Lord and Gamora just before the four of them were captured and put into the Kyln, where they also met Drax the Destroyer. Groot's bestfriend is Rocket.

Star-Lord

Peter Jason Quill is a Celestial-Human hybrid who was abducted from Earth in 1988 by the Yondu Ravager Clan and raised as one of their members, eventually building a reputation as the notorious intergalactic outlaw Star-Lord. He wears a suit that grants augmented strength and durability and the ability to travel through space. He uses an "Element Gun", a special meta-pistol capable of projecting one of the four elements (air, earth, fire and water). Star-Lord shares a psychic link with his sentient space vessel, Ship.

Rocket

Subject 89P13 is a raccoon who was genetically and cybernetically modified by High Evolutionary, giving him enhanced intelligence and the ability to speak. Naming himself Rocket, he befriended fellow captive Halfworlders: Lylla, Teefs and Floor, with the four believing they would soon be allowed to live in peace on a new planet. He is also a skilled marksman, pilot, and planner.
